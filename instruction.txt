//description project

The direction of this site is tourism within the country or city.

The project consists of 9 pages:
index.html - main page
about.html - company page
booking.html - tour booking page
certificates.html - company certificates
checkout.html - tour payment
contacts.html - company contacts
reviews.html - company reviews
tour-page.html - detailed tour page
tour-payment.html - tour payment options


Other files:
main.css - site styling along with media queries for responsive display
common.js - javascript and jquery
img - image files

Most of the icons are implemented using sprite.svg for ease of coloring. Therefore, they are displayed only on the hosting.
